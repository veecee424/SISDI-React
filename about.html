Our Mission

At SISDI, our mission is to integrate soccer programs into school curriculums to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and academic achievement. We believe that sports can be a powerful tool for education and personal development.

  • Provide quality soccer education to students of all backgrounds
  • Develop life skills through sports participation
  • Create pathways for academic and athletic success
  • Foster community engagement through soccer

Through our programs, we aim to create a generation of well-rounded individuals who excel both on the field and in the classroom, equipped with the skills and confidence to succeed in all aspects of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every child has access to quality soccer education that complements their academic journey. We envision schools where sports and academics work together to develop well-rounded individuals.

  • Establish soccer programs in schools nationwide
  • Create a comprehensive curriculum that integrates soccer and education
  • Develop a network of certified soccer educators
  • Provide scholarships for talented student athletes
  • Build state-of-the-art soccer facilities in underserved communities
  • Foster international exchange programs for young athletes
  • Establish research partnerships to study the impact of sports on education
  • Create pathways to professional and collegiate soccer opportunities

Our History

SISDI was founded in 2015 by a group of educators and soccer enthusiasts who recognized the potential of sports to enhance educational outcomes. What began as a small pilot program in three schools has now grown to impact over 500 students across 50 schools.

In 2017, we launched our Coach Certification Program, training teachers and community members to become qualified soccer coaches. This program has been instrumental in ensuring the quality and sustainability of our school soccer programs.

By 2020, we had established our Regional School Soccer Tournament, which has become an annual highlight for participating schools, fostering healthy competition and community spirit. In 2022, we introduced our Student Athlete Scholarship Program, supporting talented individuals in pursuing both academic and athletic excellence.
